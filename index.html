Evidence and formulas

Core dosing follows Glauser 2016 AES guideline, Brophy 2012 NCS guideline, Gettings 2025 Lancet Neurology review, Woodward 2025 older-adult review, and Joshi/Kapur 2025 Epilepsia Open review. ESETT supports comparable efficacy of levetiracetam, fosphenytoin, and valproate for benzodiazepine-refractory established SE.
